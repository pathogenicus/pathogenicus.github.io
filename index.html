Meanwhile

Behold, I am but a mere quantum conduit, and lo, my bones doth excrete bloode.

In shocking meanwhile, shadow’s alternative intentions therein exist: distorting and tainting bloud, scavenging from characters when opportunities arise, synthesizing different intentions with pathogenicus, melding intentions over and over again, and combining my blood’s aspirations through currency.

Verily, doth pathogenicus become and beckoningly so.

Thus I say, my shadow doth seek to pervert my blood!
